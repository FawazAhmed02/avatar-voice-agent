The hospital provides twenty four hour emergency and pharmacy services for all patients and visitors. Emergency consultation charges vary depending on the severity of the medical condition, while the pharmacy remains open throughout the day and night to ensure uninterrupted access to medicines and emergency supplies.

The Cardiology Department provides consultation and diagnostic services for heart related conditions. The cardiology specialist is available from Monday to Friday between 9 AM and 1 PM. Patients experiencing chest pain, high blood pressure, or other cardiac symptoms are encouraged to schedule an appointment in advance.

The Orthopedic Department specializes in bone, joint, and muscle related treatments. Orthopedic consultations are available on weekdays from 2 PM to 6 PM. The department also provides fracture management, physiotherapy referrals, and post surgery follow up care.

The Pediatrics clinic offers healthcare services for infants, children, and teenagers. Pediatric consultations are available daily from 10 AM to 8 PM including Saturdays. The department provides vaccination services, routine checkups, and treatment for common childhood illnesses.

The hospital laboratory provides diagnostic testing and sample collection services every day from 7 AM until 10 PM. Common laboratory services include blood tests, urine analysis, COVID screening, and routine diagnostic panels. Most reports are available digitally through the patient portal.

MRI and radiology services are available daily between 9 AM and 9 PM. MRI scans require prior appointment booking, while X ray and ultrasound services are available for walk in patients depending on queue availability.

Patients can book appointments through the hospital reception desk, online portal, or self service kiosk. Walk in patients are also accepted depending on doctor availability and current queue capacity.

The hospital accepts multiple payment methods including cash, debit cards, credit cards, and digital wallet payments. Major insurance providers including Jubilee Insurance, EFU Health, and State Life are also accepted for eligible treatments and consultations.

Visitor timings are from 5 PM to 8 PM daily. Visitors are encouraged to follow hospital safety guidelines and maintain silence in patient care areas. Masks are mandatory in intensive care units and recommended throughout the hospital premises.

Patients seeking hospital admission must provide a valid CNIC, previous medical records, and insurance information where applicable. The admissions department operates twenty four hours and assists patients with room allocation, billing, and admission formalities.

The vaccination center provides childhood and adult immunization services from Monday to Saturday between 9 AM and 5 PM. Vaccines for hepatitis, influenza, tetanus, and routine childhood immunizations are available.

Parking facilities are available in Basement Levels 1 and 2 for visitors and patients. Parking charges are calculated on an hourly basis, and valet parking is available during peak hospital hours.
