WaveTec InfoPoint — Smart Lobby Kiosk
======================================

Welcome to WaveTec InfoPoint, the intelligent self-service information kiosk
serving WaveTec Tower, 1 Innovation Drive, Silicon Bay, CA 94025.

ABOUT WAVETEC INFOPOINT
------------------------
WaveTec InfoPoint is an AI-powered lobby kiosk designed to help visitors,
employees, and guests quickly find the information they need. The kiosk
supports voice interaction, touchscreen navigation, and printed directions.
Our system operates entirely offline to protect your privacy — no voice data
is sent to external servers.

-------------------
Monday through Friday:  8:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Saturday:               10:00 AM – 4:00 PM

After-hours emergencies: contact Building Security at extension 0 or

SERVICES OFFERED
-----------------
1. Visitor Registration
   Check in for appointments, receive visitor badges, and notify your host.
   Bring a government-issued photo ID. Pre-registered visitors can use the
   Fast Check-In lane (touch the star icon on the kiosk home screen).

2. Wayfinding & Directions
   Search for any office, meeting room, amenity, or department within
   WaveTec Tower. The kiosk can print a floor map or send directions to
   your mobile phone via SMS (charges may apply from your carrier).

3. Building Directory
   Browse the full employee and department directory. Filter by floor,
   department name, or employee name. Privacy mode hides personal phone
   extensions for security.

4. Meeting Room Booking
   Check real-time meeting room availability and book a room for up to
   two hours (employee badge required). Rooms are located on floors 3–8.
   Amenities vary by room: whiteboards, video conferencing, and catering
   can be arranged through the Facilities desk on Floor 2.

5. Concierge Services
   Request a taxi or rideshare pickup from the North Entrance drop-off zone.
   Order lobby refreshments (coffee, water, snacks) delivered to your
   meeting room. Luggage storage is available at the concierge desk,
   Lobby Level, Suite 1A.

6. Accessibility Assistance
   WaveTec InfoPoint supports screen-reader mode, high-contrast display,
   and voice-only navigation. Press the blue Accessibility button at the
   bottom-left of the kiosk screen at any time to switch modes.
   A human concierge is always on duty Monday–Friday 9 AM–5 PM.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------

Q: Where is parking?
A: Visitor parking is in the underground garage, Level B1–B3. Enter from
   Innovation Drive (south side). The first 90 minutes are free; after that
   the rate is $4 per hour capped at $20 per day. Electric vehicle charging
   stations are available on Level B1, spaces EV-01 through EV-12.

Q: How do I reach a specific company or tenant?
A: Use the Building Directory service on this kiosk, or ask me by saying
   the company name out loud. I will display their floor, suite number,
   and main contact number.

Q: Is there Wi-Fi for visitors?
A: Yes. Connect to the network named "WaveTec-Guest". The password
   changes weekly; ask reception or request it here on the kiosk under
   Services → Guest Wi-Fi.

Q: Where is the nearest coffee shop?
A: The WaveTec Café is on the Lobby Level, open 7 AM–6 PM weekdays.
   The nearest external coffee shop is Brewed Awakening, 0.2 miles north
   on Innovation Drive (a 4-minute walk).

Q: What should I do in an emergency?
A: In case of fire or medical emergency, dial 911 immediately, then
   Building Security at extension 0. Emergency exits are located at the
   north and south ends of every floor. Assembly point is the Innovation
   Drive plaza, 50 metres from the main entrance.
